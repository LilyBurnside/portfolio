About me

I’m Lillian and I’m a web developer based out of Dallas, Texas. While web development is a new endeavor for me I have always loved learning, I learn languages (like the ones humans speak) as a hobby and have taught myself knitting as well!

When I graduated from the University of North Texas I realized the path I chose for myself was not one of continued learning and I knew that was not the right fit for me. One day when I was playing video games I just thought “Wait, I could do that!” and while learning to code to make games didn’t stick around long it did spark my interest in what else coding can do. Through my experiences in school and work I have grown to love working with teams where people are passionate about the work they do, and developer teams are some of the most passionate teams out there!

Today, I am learning to make web apps and websites like you’ll see below!
