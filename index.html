About me

I’m Lillian and I’m a web developer based out of Dallas, Tx. While web development is a new endeavor for me I have always loved learning, I learn languages (like the ones humans speak) as a hobby and have taught myself knitting as well!

One day when I was playing video games I just thought “Wait I could do that!” and while learning to code to make games didn’t stick around long it did spark my interest in what else coding can do.

Today, I am learning to make web apps and websites like you’ll see below!
